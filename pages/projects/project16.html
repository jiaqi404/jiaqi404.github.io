Procedurally Generated Sandy Terrain

I created a procedural node-based sandy terrain using Houdini. The process began with a randomly drawn curve, which served as the foundation for the terrain. To enhance its natural appearance, I added noise and erosion effects. Subsequently, I extracted various masks based on height and slope to aid in crafting realistic textures for the terrain.
