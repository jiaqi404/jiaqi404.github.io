I Bubble You Guess

Bubble themed interactive 3D pictionary game

"I Bubble You Guess" is an interactive 3D Pictionary game that brings creativity, teamwork, and fun to new levels. Imagine diving into a vibrant bubble world where you create 3D objects with bubbles for your friends to guess! Whether you're the artist or the guesser, you're guaranteed endless opportunities for laughter and bonding with friends. Let your imagination know no bounds in this creative and interactive game experience.

"I Bubble You Guess" is made during Global Game Jam Hong Kong 2025, and we are honored to receive the Most Unique Concept Award presented by Kowloon & Kepler! Check our GGJ webpage for more details.

Bubble Shader

I developed the bubble shader, primarily using Unity's ShaderGraph to create it. The shader allows for several adjustable parameters to achieve the desired visual effects. The _fresnelPower parameter controls the Fresnel coefficient, which affects the width of the bubble's edge, giving it a more pronounced or subtle outline. The _addAlpha parameter is used to adjust the transparency of the bubble, allowing for variations in how see-through it appears. Additionally, the blendOpacity_ parameter modifies the intensity of the iridescent halo effect, simulating the thin-film interference that gives bubbles their characteristic colorful sheen.
