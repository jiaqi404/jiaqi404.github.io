Cosmic Rhythm

Generative Art

"Cosmic Rhythm" is an exploration of the meditative experience, where the central white sphere serves as a symbolic representation of a celestial body. Encircling this sphere, a mesmerizing dance of irregularly swirling particles mirrors the rhythmic heartbeat of this celestial entity, inviting viewers to immerse themselves in a harmonious cosmic meditation.
