Procedurally Generated PBR Tiles Material

I created a procedural, node-based tile material using Substance 3D Designer. This involved generating a comprehensive set of texture maps, including base color, height, metallic, normal, smoothness, translucency, and ambient occlusion (AO). After completing the material, I packaged it into an SBSAR file, which I then imported into Unity. Within Unity, the material is highly versatile, allowing for dynamic adjustments to its color, dirt level, and more. This flexibility enables the creation of a wide variety of fabric appearances, enhancing the visual richness and customization options in the project.
