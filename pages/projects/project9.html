Godliness

Game Development

"Godliness" is a single-player action game based on Unity for the PC platform, co-developed by me and two other classmates as the final project for the "Game Development" course. My primary responsibilities included concept design, level design, scene construction and gameplay design.

Below is a demonstration of the first-level model in the game. I used Blender for modeling, UV unwrapping, and texturing work. All assets were created by me.
