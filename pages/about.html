Hello!
I'm Jiaqi.

I am an interdisciplinary creator with a passion for games, technology, and art. I am pursuing a Master of Science in Multimedia and Entertainment Technology at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, with an engineering background in Digital Media Technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My goal is to become a technical artist, combining code and art to create stunning visual and interactive experiences.

I create for the future. I am currently enthusiastic about exploring real-time rendering, procedural content generation, and artificial intelligence technologies in games. I participated in the Global Game Jam 2025, where the game I developed won the "Most Innovative Concept Award." During my internship at the PolyU - NVIDIA Joint Research Center, I optimized the MR rendering pipeline for the Meta Quest 3 and integrated an AI voice interaction module, making virtual worlds more realistic and engaging.

Outside of my professional field, I am also an avid gamer. I enjoy the philosophical reflections and TRPG narrative style of "Disco Elysium," the classic detective puzzle experience of "Return of the Obra Dinn," and the emotional storytelling and gameplay of "Celeste." I often play League of Legends: Wild Rift and closely follow the esports events of League of Legends.
