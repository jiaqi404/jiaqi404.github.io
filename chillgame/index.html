关于我们

About Us

Chill Game 工作室成立于2024年，我们结缘于香港理工大学 MSc in Innovative Multimedia Entertainment 专业。《猫猫直聘》是我们的首款游戏，也是我们的研究生毕设作品。

我们是一个非全职的、充满激情和创意的独立游戏开发团队，团队所有人都有一份全职工作，做游戏只是我们的业余爱好。我们专注于开发具有独特玩法和艺术风格的游戏，致力于为玩家带来轻松愉快且引人入胜的游戏体验。

Chill Game Studio was established in 2024, and we met at the MSc in Innovative Multimedia Entertainment program at Hong Kong PolyU. "Meow Recruit" is our first game and also our graduate project.

We are a non-full-time, passionate, and creative indie game development team. All team members have a full-time job, and making games is just our hobby. We focus on developing games with unique gameplay and art styles, aiming to provide players with a relaxing and engaging gaming experience.

猫猫直聘

Meow Recruit

《猫猫直聘》是一款以职场为主题的休闲策略游戏。你将化身动物公司的HR，通过招募员工、安排工位、优化团队、组织培训和建设设施，带领公司达成业绩目标。你是真正的HR大师吗？

Meow Recruit is a casual strategy game where you play as HR in an animal company. Recruit employees, arrange workstations, optimize your team, and build facilities to maximize company's profits. Are you the real HR master?
